Welcome to Grosvenor

Urban Property

We are an international property developer, manager and investor with a track record of over 340 years. Improving property and places across many of the world’s leading cities, we promote sustainability within the built environment, enhancing the wellbeing of our customers and communities. 

Read more

Food & AgTech

We are an international investor in food and agriculture businesses. Rethinking and re-shaping how food is grown, produced, distributed and consumed we work to help deliver a better food system - enhancing human health, our environment and enabling producers to thrive. 

Read more

Rural Estates

We are responsible for the  long-term stewardship  of  three  rural estates in the UK. Working to protect, enhance, and restore sensitive environmental habitats, we work to improve  local  property and places, contributing to the economic, social, and environmental wellbeing of the communities we are part of.
